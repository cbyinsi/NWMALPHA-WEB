Welcome to FurMeal Log (the "App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use FurMeal Log. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We may collect information you actively provide when using the App, including:

  • Account information (if you create an account): email address, display name.

  • Feeding logs and notes you create (date/time, food type, portion, notes).

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information, such as:

  • Usage data: feature usage, timestamps, and interaction events to help improve the App.
  • Device information: device model, operating system version, app version, and unique device identifiers.

1.3 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to store preferences and analytics data. These technologies do not typically contain personal information unless you provide it.

We use the information we collect to:
